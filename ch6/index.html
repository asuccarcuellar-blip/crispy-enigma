Enjoy Nature in Luxury

Pacific Trails Resort on the California North Coast offers panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean. Our guests experience an opulent camping adventure in sumptuousy appointed private yurts featuring a gas fireplace, king-size bed, and private deck.

  • Unwind in the heated outdoor pool and whirpool
  • Explore the coast on your own or join our guided tours
  • Start the day with a complimentary cooked-to-order breakfast
  • Relaz in lodge while enjoying complimentary appetizers and beverages
  • Savor the nightly fine dining with an ocean view
