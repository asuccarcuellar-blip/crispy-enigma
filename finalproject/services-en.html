Who Is It For?

Our services are primarily aimed at Spanish-speaking women in the Boston area. We also offer our services to other vulnerable populations seeking to improve their mental health through exploration and understanding of their family history. La Casa en el Arbol (The Tree House) also provides services to public and private organizations that want to positively impact the populations under their care.
