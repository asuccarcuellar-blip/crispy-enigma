La Casa en el Arbol Foundation (The Tree House)

We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit foundation promoting the wellbeing and mental health of women in the Boston area through conscious exploration of family history, emotional biodecoding, and other alternative methodologies.

Support Our Work

What We Do

We provide support to women seeking to heal and grow in community. Here you will find a safe space to explore your emotions, share experiences, and discover tools for emotional wellbeing.

Our Services

Individual conscious conversation sessions
Personal growth lectures and workshops
Training for public and private organizations
Dissemination of alternative methodologies for mental and emotional wellbeing

How to Receive Our Support

1. Contact Us
2. We evaluate your eligibility
3. Receive your service and improve your mental and emotional wellbeing

Donations

We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible and directly contribute to the wellbeing and health of vulnerable communities in the Boston area and the East Coast of the United States.
