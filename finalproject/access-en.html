How to Receive Support

At La Casa en el Árbol (The Tree House) we ensure that your first contact is clear, safe, and welcoming. Here we guide you step by step through the process.

Steps to Receive Our Support

Step 1: Initial Contact

Send an email or fill out our form. Our team will respond within 24–48 hours to schedule the first meeting.

Step 2: Needs Assessment

During the first session, we evaluate your needs, expectations, and goals to design a personalized support plan.

Step 3: Start of Support

We begin the sessions according to your personalized plan, with continuous monitoring and adjustments based on your progress and feedback.

General Requirements

To access our services, you must be over 18 years old, reside in the Boston area or surrounding communities, and be available for scheduled sessions. No prior experience or clinical diagnosis is required.

What to Expect

Our support combines individual sessions and group workshops. Each participant receives a personalized plan and continuous follow-up. Session duration ranges from 60 to 90 minutes, and the process may vary depending on individual needs and goals.

Important Clarifications

This support does not replace medical attention or emergency services. In case of crisis, we recommend contacting specialized mental health services or local emergency services.
