Support Our Work

Your contribution helps La Casa en el Árbol (The Tree House) continue providing wellbeing and emotional health support to women and vulnerable communities in Boston, Massachusetts, and the United States. Each donation enables us to offer emotional care sessions, retreats, workshops, and educational meetings, reaching those who need it most.

Why Donate?

As a 501(c)(3) foundation, every contribution we receive is used exclusively for programs supporting wellbeing, self-knowledge, and personal development. With your support, we can cover fees for specialized professionals, provide access to sessions for those who cannot afford them, and expand our educational and community activities. Your donation creates a direct and tangible impact on the lives of those we serve.

Transparency and Trust

La Casa en el Árbol (The Tree House) is a registered 501(c)(3) foundation, fully compliant with legal and tax requirements for nonprofit organizations. We maintain clear reports on the use of funds and provide accountability to our donors through annual reports and regular updates on our activities.
