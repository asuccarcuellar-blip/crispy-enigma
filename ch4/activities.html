Activities at Pacific Trails

Hicking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of Hicking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our gided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use.

While anytume is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week.
